CXP and CDFP connectors facilitate high bandwidth with multiple channels, ideal for large data transfers. CFP (C Form-factor Pluggable) connectors enable high-speed interfaces, while SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) connectors provide flexibility for lower-speed connections.
