<p><p>The airport luggage trailers market includes two main types: enclosed and open trailers. Enclosed trailers are fully covered, providing protection from weather, theft, and damage, making them ideal for secure and long-distance transport. Open trailers, on the other hand, are exposed and lack walls or roofs, offering easier loading and unloading but less protection. Both are essential for efficient luggage handling at airports, with enclosed trailers preferred for security and open trailers for quick, short-distance operations.</p></p>
