<p><strong>The Analog Multipliers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Quadrant</li><li>Two Quadrant</li><li>Four Quadrant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog multipliers are classified into three market types based on input voltage behavior: </p><p>1. **Single Quadrant**: These multipliers accept input signals in the first quadrant only, where both inputs are positive, typically used in basic signal processing. </p><p>2. **Two Quadrant**: Capable of accepting inputs in the first and fourth quadrants, they can handle both positive and negative signals, ideal for applications like audio processing.</p><p>3. **Four Quadrant**: These multipliers accept inputs across all four quadrants, accommodating any combination of positive and negative signals, often used in advanced control systems and instrumentation.</p></p>
