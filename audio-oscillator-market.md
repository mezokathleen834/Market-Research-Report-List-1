<p><p>The audio oscillator market can be categorized into low audio and high audio segments based on frequency ranges and applications. Low audio oscillators operate within frequencies typically below 20 kHz, catering to applications like bass synthesizers and subwoofer drivers, where deep sound production is essential. High audio oscillators, on the other hand, function above 20 kHz and are utilized in applications like ultrasonic testing and high-frequency sound generation, serving industries such as medical and industrial diagnostics.</p></p>
