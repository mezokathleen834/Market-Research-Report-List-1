<p><p>The Automotive Multi-wheel Drive (MWD) market includes Manual Multi-wheel Drive (manual MWD) and Automatic Multi-wheel Drive (automatic MWD) systems. Manual MWD requires driver intervention to engage or disengage the system, offering greater control and customization for off-road or challenging terrains. Automatic MWD, on the other hand, operates seamlessly without driver input, using sensors and algorithms to distribute power to wheels as needed, enhancing convenience and performance in varying conditions. Both systems cater to diverse driving needs, balancing control, efficiency, and adaptability.</p></p>
