<p><p>The **Automotive Steering Device Market** includes three main types: **Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)**, which uses hydraulic pressure for assistance; **Electro-hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)**, combining hydraulic systems with electric motors for efficiency; and **Electric Power Steering (EPS)**, relying solely on electric motors for precise control and fuel savings. HPS is robust but less efficient, EHPS offers a balance, and EPS is lightweight, energy-efficient, and increasingly popular in modern vehicles due to its adaptability and reduced environmental impact.</p></p>
