<p><p>The Automotive Steering Lock System market includes various types designed to enhance vehicle security. **T-Lock** systems feature a T-shaped mechanism that locks the steering wheel, preventing rotation. **Top Hook Lock** systems use a hook-like design that secures the steering wheel from the top, offering robust protection. **Baseball Lock** systems employ a cylindrical, baseball bat-like lock that fits into the steering wheel, immobilizing it.
