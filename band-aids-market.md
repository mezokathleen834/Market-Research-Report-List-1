<p><p>The Band-Aids market encompasses various types, notably the Flexible Fabric Bandage and Cohesive Fixation Bandage. In contrast, Cohesive Fixation Bandages are non-adhesive and stick to themselves, providing secure support for dressings or immobilization without irritating the skin. Both types cater to diverse medical needs, enhancing wound care and recovery.</p></p>
