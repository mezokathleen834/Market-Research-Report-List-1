<p><p>Biosurgery encompasses a range of products that enhance surgical procedures and promote healing. Surgical sealants and adhesive barriers prevent leaks and reduce fluid accumulation. Hemostatic agents control bleeding during operations. Soft-tissue attachments facilitate the secure joining of tissues, while biological meshes support tissue repair. Allografts and xenografts provide biological tissue from human or animal sources for grafting, promoting healing. Bone-graft substitutions offer synthetic or biological materials to replace or augment bone, playing a crucial role in orthopedic and reconstructive surgeries.</p></p>
