<p><p>The breakdown truck market includes various types designed for vehicle recovery. **Boom Truck with Underlift** features a hydraulic boom and underlift for versatile towing. **Wheel-lift Tow Truck** uses a metal yoke to lift vehicles by their wheels, ideal for tight spaces. **Integrated Tow Truck** combines boom and wheel-lift systems for heavy-duty recovery. **Flatbed with Wheel-Lift** offers a flatbed for secure transport and a wheel-lift for added functionality. **Lift Flatbed** uses a hydraulic lift to load vehicles onto the flatbed, ensuring minimal damage during transport. Each type caters to specific towing needs.</p></p>
