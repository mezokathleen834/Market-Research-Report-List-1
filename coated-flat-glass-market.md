<p><p>The coated flat glass market encompasses several types, including ordinary flat glass, float glass, and rolled glass. Ordinary flat glass, often used in windows and displays, offers basic transparency. Float glass is produced by floating molten glass on tin, resulting in a smooth surface ideal for high-quality applications like facades and mirrors. Rolled glass is created by passing molten glass through rollers, giving it texture and added durability, commonly used in architectural and decorative applications.</p></p>
