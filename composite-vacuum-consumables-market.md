<p><p>The composite vacuum consumables market encompasses various types that play crucial roles in the composite manufacturing process. Vacuum bagging films are used to create airtight seals, facilitating resin infusion. Release films provide a smooth surface for easy demolding. Peel ply enables excellent bonding by promoting resin adhesion while preventing contamination. Breathers and bleeders facilitate airflow and resin transfer during curing. Other consumables may include adhesives and sealants, enhancing efficiency and quality in composite production.</p></p>
