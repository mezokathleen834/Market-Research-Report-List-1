<p><strong>The Concrete Containing Polymers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer Concrete (PC)</li><li>Latex-modified Concrete (LMC)</li><li>Polymer-impregnated Concrete (PIC)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Polymer Concrete (PC)** market involves concrete where polymers replace cement as the binder, offering high strength, durability, and chemical resistance. **Latex-modified Concrete (LMC)** incorporates latex polymers into the mix, enhancing flexibility, adhesion, and crack resistance, ideal for overlays and repairs. **Polymer-impregnated Concrete (PIC)** is created by impregnating cured concrete with liquid polymers, significantly improving strength, impermeability, and durability. The **Others** category includes niche applications like fiber-reinforced polymer concrete, tailored for specialized uses requiring unique performance characteristics. Each type caters to specific construction needs, balancing cost and performance.</p></p>
