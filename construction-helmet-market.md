<p><p>The construction helmet market features three primary types: ABS protective helmets, HDPE protective helmets, and FRP protective helmets. ABS helmets are lightweight and impact-resistant, offering good protection and comfort. HDPE helmets are durable and resistant to chemicals and UV rays, making them suitable for various outdoor environments. FRP helmets provide superior strength and insulation, often used in high-voltage and hazardous conditions. Each type caters to different safety requirements and industry standards, ensuring worker protection on construction sites.</p></p>
