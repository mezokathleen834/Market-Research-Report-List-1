<p><ul><li>Pasting Cork Floor</li><li>Lock Cork Floor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cork floor market features two primary types: pasting cork floor and lock cork floor. Pasting cork floor involves adhesive application for installation, providing a seamless and smooth finish, ideal for residential and commercial spaces. In contrast, lock cork floor utilizes a tongue-and-groove system, allowing for easier and quicker installation without glue. This type is popular for DIY projects and renovations, offering the same aesthetic and acoustic benefits as pasting cork floors while enhancing convenience and flexibility in flooring options.</p></p>
