<p><p>The Curtain Wall Adhesive market is primarily divided into two types: acidic and neutral adhesives. Acidic adhesives utilize a chemical reaction to bond materials, often providing strong adhesion but requiring careful handling due to their corrosive nature. In contrast, neutral adhesives are less aggressive, employing a more balanced formulation that minimizes potential damage to substrates, making them suitable for various sensitive applications. Both types are essential for ensuring the durability and structural integrity of curtain wall systems in construction.</p></p>
