<p><p>Cutback bitumen is a type of asphalt that is modified with solvents to improve its flowability and ease of application. The market is categorized into three types: Rapid Curing (RC), Medium Curing (MC), and Slow Curing (SC). RC cutback evaporates quickly, allowing for fast setting, ideal for colder climates. MC offers moderate evaporation, providing a balance for various applications. SC cutback has a slower evaporation rate, enhancing workability in hot conditions and longer application times, addressing specific project needs.</p></p>
