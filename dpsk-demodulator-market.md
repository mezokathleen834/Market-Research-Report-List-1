<p><p>The DPSK demodulator market is categorized into three types: tunable, passive, and semi-tunable. Passive demodulators rely on minimal external components, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for stable applications. Semi-tunable demodulators strike a balance between the two, allowing some level of manual adjustment while maintaining a straightforward design. These distinctions cater to different application needs, from high-performance communications to cost-sensitive systems.</p></p>
