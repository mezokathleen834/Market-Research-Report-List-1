<p><p>Electric motors play a crucial role in the electric vehicle market, driving various types. Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs) combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor for improved efficiency. Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) extend this by allowing external charging for the electric motor, enabling longer electric-only travel. Pure Electric Vehicles (PEVs) rely solely on electric motors powered by batteries, offering zero emissions and greater reliance on electric energy. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences in sustainable transportation.</p></p>
