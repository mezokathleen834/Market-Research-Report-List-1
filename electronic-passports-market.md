<p><p>The electronic passport market consists of three primary types: Ordinary e-passports, Service e-passports, and Diplomatic e-passports. Ordinary e-passports are issued to regular citizens for travel purposes, featuring a digital chip that stores personal information. Service e-passports are designated for government officials on official duty, providing enhanced security and expedited processing. Diplomatic e-passports, issued to diplomats, offer additional privileges and protections during international travel, reflecting their status in foreign relations. Each type is tailored to specific user needs and security requirements.</p></p>
