<p><p>The EV battery recycling market encompasses various battery types, each with distinct characteristics. **Nickel-cadmium (Ni-Cd) batteries** are durable but less eco-friendly due to cadmium. **Nickel-metal hydride (NiMH) batteries** offer higher energy density and are safer, commonly used in hybrids. **Lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries** dominate the EV market due to high efficiency and energy density. **Lithium polymer (Li-Po) batteries** are lightweight and flexible, ideal for compact designs. **Lead-acid batteries**, though older, are cost-effective and widely recycled. Each type requires tailored recycling processes to recover valuable materials and minimize environmental impact.</p></p>
