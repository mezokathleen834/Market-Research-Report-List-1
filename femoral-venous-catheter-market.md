<p><p>The femoral venous catheter market is categorized into unipolar and bipolar femoral arterial cannulae, each serving distinct clinical needs. Unipolar femoral arterial cannulae allow for single-direction blood flow, frequently used in less complex procedures. In contrast, bipolar femoral arterial cannulae facilitate bidirectional flow, providing enhanced control during interventions. Both types are vital in critical care settings for hemodynamic monitoring and therapeutic applications, contributing to the efficiency and safety of vascular access in patients undergoing various medical treatments.</p></p>
