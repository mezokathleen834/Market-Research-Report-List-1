<p><p>Flat Panel Displays (FPD) encompass various technologies, including Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED), Liquid Crystal Display (LCD), and Plasma Display (PDP). OLED offers vibrant colors, deep blacks, and flexibility, making it popular for smartphones and high-end TVs. LCD, using liquid crystals, is efficient for various applications, including monitors and TVs, but may struggle with color contrast. PDP, while offering excellent color reproduction and wide viewing angles, has diminished in popularity due to its bulkiness and the rise of OLED and LCD technologies.</p></p>
