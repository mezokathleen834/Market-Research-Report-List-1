<p><ul><li>Single Joint</li><li>Multiple Joint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gait rehabilitation exoskeleton market is divided into two main types: single joint and multiple joint exoskeletons. Single joint exoskeletons are designed to assist movement at a specific joint, focusing on targeted rehabilitation and support, often beneficial for patients with localized impairments. In contrast, multiple joint exoskeletons facilitate movement across multiple joints simultaneously, providing comprehensive gait support and allowing for more natural movement patterns. This variety enables tailored rehabilitation solutions to meet diverse patient needs and recovery goals.</p></p>
