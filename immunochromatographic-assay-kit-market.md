<p><strong>The Immunochromatographic Assay Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sandwich Assay</li><li>Competitive Assay</li><li>Multiplex Detection Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunochromatographic assay kit market can be categorized into three main types: Sandwich Assay, Competitive Assay, and Multiplex Detection Assay. Sandwich Assays utilize two antibodies to capture and detect the target antigen, providing high specificity and sensitivity. Competitive Assays involve the competition between a labeled antigen and the target for binding sites, which can yield quantitative results. Multiplex Detection Assays allow simultaneous detection of multiple analytes within a single sample, enhancing efficiency and comprehensive diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
