<p><p>The Industrial Grade HPMC (Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose) market is segmented into three viscosity types: low, middle, and high viscosity. Low viscosity HPMC is commonly used in formulations requiring fast solubility and lower thickening power, suitable for coatings and adhesives. Middle viscosity HPMC offers balanced properties, making it versatile for applications like construction and personal care products. High viscosity HPMC provides superior thickening and gel-forming capabilities, ideal for demanding applications in pharmaceuticals and food products, where texture and stability are crucial.</p></p>
