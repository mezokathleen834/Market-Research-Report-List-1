<p><p>The industrial grade salt market consists of three main types: sea salt, well and rock salt, and lake salt. Sea salt is harvested through the evaporation of seawater and is often used in chemical processes. Well and rock salt is mined from underground deposits, commonly utilized in de-icing and as a raw material in various industries. Lake salt, derived from saline lakes, is primarily used in agriculture and chemical production. Each type serves distinct applications based on its origin and properties.</p></p>
