<p><p>Liquid Flow Screed is a self-leveling flooring material used for smooth, durable surfaces. The market includes two main types: Pumpable Calcium Sulphate Screed and Pumpable Cement Based Screed. Calcium Sulphate Screed, made from gypsum, offers fast drying, thermal efficiency, and is ideal for underfloor heating. Cement Based Screed, composed of cement and sand, provides high strength, durability, and versatility for heavy-duty applications. Both types are pumpable, ensuring efficient installation, but differ in composition, drying times, and suitability for specific project requirements.</p></p>
