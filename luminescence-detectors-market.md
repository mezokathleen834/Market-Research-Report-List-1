<p><p>Luminescence detectors are critical in various scientific applications, including biological assays and environmental monitoring. Tube luminescence detectors utilize cylindrical tubes to measure light emitted from luminescent samples, offering precise, high-throughput analysis. Conversely, microplate luminescence detectors are designed for multi-well plates, enabling simultaneous analysis of multiple samples. This enhances efficiency and scalability in experiments, particularly in drug discovery and clinical diagnostics. Both types contribute significantly to the advancement of research by providing sensitive and rapid detection of luminescent signals.</p></p>
