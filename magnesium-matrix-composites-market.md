<p><p>The Magnesium Matrix Composites market is divided into two primary types: cast and wrought. Cast magnesium composites are produced by pouring molten magnesium into molds, allowing for complex shapes and large-scale production. They are typically used in applications requiring intricate designs but may have lower strength. Wrought magnesium composites, on the other hand, involve deforming the material through processes like rolling or forging, offering improved mechanical properties and strength, making them suitable for applications in aerospace and automotive sectors.</p></p>
