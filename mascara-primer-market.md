<p><p>The mascara primer market is categorized into three main types: waterproof, thickening, and lengthening primers. Waterproof primers are designed to resist moisture and smudging, ensuring long-lasting wear even in humid conditions. Thickening primers enhance lash volume by coating each strand, creating a fuller appearance. Lengthening primers focus on extending the lashes, giving them a longer look by separating and defining each lash. Each type caters to specific consumer needs, making them popular choices for achieving desired lash effects.</p></p>
