<p><p>The metallurgical limestone market comprises several types, including dolomitic limestone, calcitic dolomite, and magnesian limestones. Dolomitic limestone, rich in magnesium and calcium carbonate, is vital in steelmaking for fluxing and enhancing the quality of the steel. Calcitic dolomite, primarily consisting of calcium carbonate, is used for its purity in metallurgical processes. Magnesian limestones, containing significant magnesium content, are crucial for specific metallurgical applications, helping to control slag composition and improve the overall efficiency of metal production.</p></p>
