<p><p>The Military Grade Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) market includes three main types: Passive Standby, Online, and Line-Interactive systems. Passive Standby UPS provides basic backup by switching to battery power during outages, suitable for less critical applications. Online UPS offers continuous power through dual conversion, ensuring no interruption in power delivery, ideal for sensitive military equipment. Line-Interactive UPS combines features of both, providing voltage regulation and battery backup, making it versatile for environments needing both efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
