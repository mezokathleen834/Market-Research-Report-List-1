<p><p>The network tester market comprises three primary types: qualification testing, certification testing, and troubleshooting testing. Qualification testing verifies that a network’s performance meets specific standards, ensuring it can handle intended loads. Certification testing goes further, validating that the network complies with industry regulations and standards. Troubleshooting testing focuses on diagnosing and resolving issues within existing network systems, helping to identify faults and inefficiencies. Together, these testing types ensure optimal network performance, compliance, and reliability.</p></p>
