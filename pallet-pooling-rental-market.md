<p><p>Pallet pooling involves sharing a network of pallets among multiple users, allowing businesses to access high-quality pallets without ownership costs. This system enhances efficiency, reduces waste, and promotes sustainability. In contrast, the pallet rental market focuses on leasing pallets for specific needs, often for shorter durations. Both models cater to different business requirements, enabling flexibility in supply chain management while minimizing the financial burden of purchasing and maintaining pallets outright. Ultimately, both support more efficient logistics operations.</p></p>
