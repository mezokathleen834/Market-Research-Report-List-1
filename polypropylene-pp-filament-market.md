<p><p>The polypropylene (PP) filament market is primarily divided into two types: polypropylene multifilament and polypropylene monofilament. Polypropylene multifilament consists of multiple strands twisted together, offering flexibility and strength, making it ideal for applications like fishing nets and textiles. In contrast, polypropylene monofilament is a single, solid strand that provides durability and is commonly used in applications such as ropes, brushes, and medical devices. Both types leverage the lightweight, chemical resistance, and cost-effectiveness of polypropylene in various industries.</p></p>
