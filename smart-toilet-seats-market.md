<p><p>The smart toilet seats market is categorized into two main heating types: storage heating and instantaneous heating. Storage heating units utilize a reservoir to preheat water for use, providing a constant supply at a set temperature, which can be energy-efficient for consistent usage. In contrast, instantaneous heating systems heat water on demand, offering quick access to warm water without the need for storage, making them compact and potentially more suitable for settings with limited space. Both types enhance user comfort and hygiene.</p></p>
