<p><p>The Steel Core Aluminum Stranded Wire (ACSR) market features several key types. ACSR consists of aluminum strands wrapped around a steel core, providing strength and conductivity, ideal for overhead power lines. ACSR/AW includes aluminum wire with a higher aluminum content for improved conductivity and reduced weight. ACSR/TW, or "Tough Wire," features additional protective layers or coatings to enhance durability and resistance to environmental factors. These variations cater to different requirements for power transmission and distribution, ensuring reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
