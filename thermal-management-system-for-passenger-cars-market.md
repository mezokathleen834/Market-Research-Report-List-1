<p><p>The Thermal Management System (TMS) for passenger cars ensures optimal operating temperatures for vehicle components. Key market types include:  </p><p>1. **Electric Fan**: Regulates airflow to cool the radiator and engine, enhancing efficiency and reducing overheating risks.  </p><p>2. **Electric Water Pump**: Circulates coolant through the engine and radiator, maintaining temperature control with precise, energy-efficient operation.  </p><p>3. **Radiator**: Dissipates heat from the coolant, preventing engine overheating and ensuring stable performance.  </p><p>4. **Thermostat**: Controls coolant flow based on temperature, optimizing engine warm-up and maintaining ideal operating conditions.  </p><p>These components collectively improve vehicle performance, fuel efficiency, and longevity.</p></p>
