<p><p>Thermocouple tubes are protective sheaths used in high-temperature environments to safeguard thermocouples. Mullite tubes offer excellent thermal shock resistance and are cost-effective for moderate temperatures. Corundum tubes, made of pure alumina, provide superior mechanical strength and high-temperature stability. High-purity alumina tubes are ideal for extreme temperatures and corrosive environments due to their exceptional chemical resistance. Mullite-bonded silicon carbide tubes combine thermal shock resistance with high thermal conductivity. Zirconia tubes excel in ultra-high-temperature applications, offering low thermal conductivity and excellent insulation properties. Each type caters to specific industrial needs based on temperature, durability, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
