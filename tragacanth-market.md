<p><p>The Tragacanth market includes two primary types: Ribbon Form Tragacanth and Flake Form Tragacanth. Ribbon Form Tragacanth is characterized by its elongated, ribbon-like structure, often used in applications requiring high viscosity and stability, such as pharmaceuticals and food additives. Flake Form Tragacanth, on the other hand, consists of smaller, flaky pieces, preferred for its ease of handling and solubility in various industries like cosmetics and textiles. Both forms are natural gums derived from the Astragalus plant, valued for their thickening and emulsifying properties.</p></p>
